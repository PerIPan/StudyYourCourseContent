What are the key differences between threat actors according to ENISA?
According to the ENISA Threat Landscape 2025, threat actors are categorized by motivation and capability:

1. State-sponsored — highest capability, geopolitical motives...
2. Cybercriminals — financially motivated, ransomware...
3. Hacktivists — ideological, DDoS and defacement...

📄 Sources:
• Threat Landscape / Lecture 2 / ENISA Report, p.14-18
• Threat Landscape / Lecture 2 / Understanding Adversaries, slide 7
